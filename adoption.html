ADOPTION FEES/WHAT'S INCLUDED


Adoption Fees: Rs.15000.0 for all dogs.All of our adoptable animals are up to date on vaccinations, spayed/neutered, de-wormed, microchipped, and free of parasites such as fleas and ticks.
Upon adoption, new pet parents are sent home with their new dog's first 6 months of heartworm prevention, a starter bag of H-E-B Heritage Ranch food, and a voucher for a free wellness exam at participating veterinarians.
If you fall in love with a heartworm positive dog, we provide treatment at no extra cost to you!

HOW TO ADOPT



START BY FILLING ADOPTION FORM


Interested in adoption? Navigate to our dog page and fill out the adoption application form to get started. Submitting this form does not mean you are required to adopt, but it does help speed up the process! You can fill out this form before you visit or at the shelter.
Begin the adoption process now by filling out the form! This form helps us make your adoption smooth and simple. ​



AT THE SHELTER - COVID-19 POLICIES


Our facilities are open 7 days a week for you to come in and meet your new potential pet. Due to COVID-19, face coverings are required to be worn over the mouth and nose at all times within our facilities. You can browse all of our available animals in Puppy Condo. One of our kennel attendants can help you find a pet that's right for you or your family.



I FOUND MY FUREVER FRIEND, NOW WHAT?


One of our kennel staff will be available to assist you with meeting with the dog of your interest. Once you've made your choice, staff will walk you through the adoption process to make sure everything goes smoothly. In most cases, you can leave with your animal the same day!
